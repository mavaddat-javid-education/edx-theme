HERA will help you learn Science.

You will experiment with a simulation and observe the results, and then answer 10 questions. If you answer incorrectly, you can ask for help. You will need to use "lightning bolts" to buy help. You will start the lesson with 300 lightning bolts.

Each question will offer three types of help: Rephrase, Break it down, Teach me.

If you answer incorrectly, you can try a new answer up to two more times.

  • Click Rephrase for a new version of the question.
    Cost is 10 lightning bolts!
  • Click Break it down to see the first step needed.
    Cost is 20 lightning bolts!
  • Click Teach me to learn more about the skill, and to see an example.
    Cost is 30 lightning bolts!
